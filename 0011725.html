Описание 0000548
(B) The player is unable to complete the 'Offer your help to Bes'
mission when killing the mission specific bandits prior to speaking with
Bes in the Garbage zone.
1. Begin a new game from the main menu.
2. Enter the Garbage zone and proceed to Bes' location.
3. After the player PDA receives the mission 'Offer your help to Bes',
circle around to the back side of the vehicle lot without speaking to Bes.
4. Kill all the bandits in the area.
5. Return to Bes and speak with him.
6. Observe the game behavior and Bes' dialogue.
The player's PDA should update to a completed mission after speaking
with Bes.
