Сводка 0011313: Players are able to duplicate other players names when entering "name /]\" in the console while in game.
Описание Players are able to duplicate other players names when entering "name /]\" in the console while in game. (see notes for more info)
1. Launch S.T.A.L.K.E.R and select Network Games from the main menu.
2. Select a multiplayer match on LAN or internet, then proceed to gameplay.
3. Once in game hit the ~ key to bring up the console.
4. type "name /]\" without the quotes to change you name.
5. Play a round, then hit tab to bring up the scores and names.
